Compliance Statement

Last Updated: January 2025

Regulatory Compliance

Harrington Capital maintains comprehensive compliance programs across all jurisdictions where we operate. Our compliance framework ensures adherence to all applicable federal and state regulations governing commercial lending and financial services.

Licensing & Registration

Harrington Capital holds all required state and federal licenses for business lending operations. We maintain active registrations with relevant regulatory authorities and comply with all disclosure and reporting requirements.

Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

We maintain a robust AML compliance program including customer identification procedures, transaction monitoring, and suspicious activity reporting in accordance with the Bank Secrecy Act and USA PATRIOT Act.

Know Your Customer (KYC)

All client relationships are subject to thorough KYC verification procedures. We collect and verify business information, beneficial ownership details, and conduct appropriate due diligence before establishing credit facilities.

Fair Lending Practices

Harrington Capital is committed to fair and equitable lending practices. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, national origin, sex, marital status, age, or any other protected characteristic in our underwriting decisions.

Data Protection & Privacy

We comply with all applicable data protection regulations including state privacy laws. Client information is protected through encryption, secure storage, and strict access controls. See our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

Transparent Disclosures

We provide clear and comprehensive disclosures of all credit facility terms, including interest rates, fees, payment schedules, and material terms. Clients receive full documentation before entering into any credit agreement.
