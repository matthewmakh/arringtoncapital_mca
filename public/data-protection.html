Data Protection & Security

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Data Security Commitment

Harrington Capital is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all client data through industry-leading security practices and compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

Encryption

We employ multiple layers of encryption:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive communications
  • Encrypted database storage

Access Controls

Access to client data is restricted through role-based access controls, multi-factor authentication, and the principle of least privilege. All access is logged and monitored.

Infrastructure Security

Our systems are hosted in secure, SOC 2 Type II certified data centers with redundant systems, regular backups, and disaster recovery capabilities.

Monitoring and Incident Response

We maintain 24/7 security monitoring and have established incident response procedures to quickly identify and address any security threats.

Compliance

Our data protection practices comply with:

  • Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA)
  • Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA)
  • State data protection laws
  • Industry best practices and standards

Data Retention

We retain client data only as long as necessary for business purposes and legal compliance, after which it is securely disposed of through approved methods.
