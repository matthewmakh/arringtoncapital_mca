About Harrington Capital

Harrington Capital is a New York-based alternative credit fund specializing in direct-to-business lending solutions. We provide institutional-grade capital access to growing companies through revolving credit lines, term loans, and structured financing products—all without broker intermediaries or traditional banking bureaucracy.

Founded on principles of transparency and alignment, Harrington Capital operates as a direct lender, meaning businesses work directly with our underwriting team and receive custom-structured terms designed around their specific cash flow patterns. Unlike broker-originated financing that prioritizes commission-based products, we present all viable options upfront and structure agreements that serve your long-term interests.

Our underwriting process evaluates revenue performance and business fundamentals rather than relying solely on personal credit scores. We specialize in revenue-based lending for established businesses seeking flexible working capital, equipment financing, expansion capital, or debt consolidation through our loan buyout program.

Every financing structure is customized before commitment—we analyze your cash flow, identify optimal repayment schedules, and present detailed terms for review. This ensures the capital solution actually works for your business model rather than creating payment strain.

Our Financing Solutions

Harrington Capital offers a comprehensive suite of direct lending products designed for established businesses with consistent revenue. Each solution is structured to align with your cash flow patterns and business objectives.

Revolving Line of Credit (RLOC)

Our primary product offering—a flexible credit facility that functions like a business credit card with institutional terms. Access capital on-demand, pay interest only on amounts drawn, and maintain a revolving credit line that replenishes as you repay.

  • Revenue-based approval—no personal guarantee required for qualifying businesses
  • Competitive interest rates determined through comprehensive underwriting analysis
  • Flexible draw schedule—access funds as needed, when needed
  • Minimum monthly payment of 2% of outstanding balance (or optional daily/weekly payments)
  • Zero prepayment penalties—pay down principal anytime without fees
  • Unrestricted use of funds for working capital, expansion, equipment, or refinancing
  • Rapid funding within 24-48 hours post-approval with streamlined documentation
Ideal For: Businesses with consistent monthly revenue seeking flexible working capital access. Perfect for managing cash flow gaps, seasonal inventory needs, or opportunistic expansion without the constraints of traditional term loans.

Loan Buyout & Debt Consolidation

Consolidate multiple high-cost merchant cash advances, factor agreements, or short-term loans into a single, affordable payment structure. Our buyout program eliminates ongoing broker contact, reduces total monthly obligations, and provides clear, transparent terms going forward.

  • Complete buyout of existing high-cost financing obligations
  • Consolidation into single monthly payment with improved cash flow impact
  • No debt consolidation fees or origination charges
  • Elimination of broker interference and unwanted refinancing solicitations
  • Potential early payoff incentives for businesses in strong financial position
  • Transparent amortization schedule with no hidden fees or balloon payments
Ideal For: Businesses currently managing multiple financing products with high monthly payments. Particularly beneficial for companies experiencing cash flow pressure from merchant cash advances or factor agreements seeking consolidation and payment relief.

Structured Capital & Term Financing

Custom-structured financing solutions for businesses with specific capital deployment needs. We offer term loans, equipment financing, credit card processing programs, and working capital facilities structured around your business's unique requirements and repayment capacity.

  • Fixed-term loan products with customized amortization schedules
  • Equipment and asset-based financing for capital expenditures
  • Credit card processing programs with competitive interchange rates
  • Working capital term loans for specific projects or initiatives
  • Dedicated account manager for ongoing relationship management
  • Custom payment structures aligned to seasonal revenue patterns
  • Flexible terms from 12 to 60 months based on use case
Ideal For: Businesses with specific financing needs requiring fixed-term structures—equipment purchases, facility expansion, technology upgrades, or project-based capital requirements where revolving credit may not be optimal.

How Our Financing Process Works

Harrington Capital's streamlined application and approval process is designed for efficiency while maintaining thorough underwriting standards. Our revenue-based approach evaluates your business fundamentals rather than relying solely on personal credit metrics.

1

Application & Documentation

Submit your business information through our secure online application. Our underwriting team requires standard business documentation: bank statements, tax returns, financial statements, and business verification. Initial review typically completes within 24 hours of receiving complete documentation.

2

Underwriting & Approval

Our analysis focuses on revenue consistency, monthly deposits, business tenure, and industry performance. Approval decisions are based on business fundamentals—typically $50,000+ in monthly revenue with 12+ months operating history. Credit lines are sized based on average monthly revenue and business stability factors.

3

Offer Review & Terms

Receive a detailed funding proposal with transparent terms: credit limit, interest rate, repayment structure, and fees. No obligation to accept. Our team reviews terms with you to ensure alignment with your cash flow patterns before you commit. Custom repayment schedules available upon request.

4

Funding & Account Access

After accepting terms and completing final documentation, funds are typically disbursed within 24-48 hours via ACH transfer to your business account. Access your client portal for real-time balance monitoring, draw requests, payment scheduling, and document management. Future draws processed same-day through your dedicated account manager.
